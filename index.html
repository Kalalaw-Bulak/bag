JUST IN: University of Cebu (UC) civil engineering graduate, Francis Charles P. Lauta, received his brand-new car today, December 10, 2024, at the UC main activity center for topping the November 2024 and December 2024 Civil Engineer Licensure Exam (CELE). UC also awarded the topnotchers of the November 2024 Customs Brokers Licensure Exam, Anna Dominique Castañeda, Top 5 (UC Main), Mary Christy Miñoza, Top 8 (UCLM), Simon Spencer Solon, Top 10 (UCLM) with cash incentives. Present during the awarding ceremony were the university Chairman, Atty. Augusto W. Go and the deans of the College of Customs Administration and Engineering. UC is known for giving cars or cash incentives to its graduates who land in the Top 1 of licensure examinations and Top 10 in physician and bar examinations. Congratulations, TOPNOTCHERS!
